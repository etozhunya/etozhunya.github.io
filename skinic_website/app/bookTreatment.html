    In most cases, the treatment effect occurs after 1-3 days. After 7-14 days the full effect (and thus the treated area) reveals itself. It rarely takes longer before the result appears. However, according to specialist information, the maximum effect occurs after 5-6 weeks.

    The effect generally lasts 3-6 months. However, this is on an individual basis and also depends on the treated area. Avoiding a mask-like face, also called ‘frozen face’, is contrary to the desire for a prolonged botulinum effect. We recommend moderate doses of botulinum for a natural result. Before treatment, professional advice and information is a must. Furthermore, a strategic treatment plan for anti-wrinkle treatment or prevention needs to be worked out together with the client.
